About Us

We are a small family run business on the border between Leicestershire and Rutland and situated approximately 2 miles from Uppingham, down a little lane.

When we first moved to the property there was nothing but fields. Over time we shaped the farm so it can provide a secluded relaxing spot to come and unwind.

The property offers a fishing lake, a tea shop and a five van caravan site. (Please note we are no longer offering B&B)

We thoroughly love the place and we hope that you do too! So come on down and see for yourself just how beautiful Leicestershire can be!
